Quiz 3 - Question 1

Use internal JavaScript to implement the features below:

  • When you click any square, h1 must change to "You just clicked blue/green/red square" (depending on the clicked color).
  • When you click any of the first two squares, the color of that clicked square must change to a darker color, the other one must change to a lighter color.
  • When you click the red square, the red square must disappear and the other two squares must change to the original colors;
  • When you click any of the first two squares, the red square must re-appear.
  • Creating only one function for all events will receive 10 bonus points.
